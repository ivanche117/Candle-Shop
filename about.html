Our Story

Lumi was born from a passion for creating candles that transform any space into a haven of warmth. Founded in 2024, we are dedicated to using the finest ingredients and sustainable practices to craft candles that not only smell divine but also uplift your spirit. Each candle tells a story of artistry and care that defines Lumi. What started as a small home project has grown into a beloved brand cherished by many.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every Lumi candle is carefully made from natural ingredients. We use eco-friendly soy wax, premium essential oils, and lead-free wicks to ensure a clean, long-lasting burn. Additionally, we are committed to sustainability and use recyclable packaging products to reduce our environmental footprint. Our artisans meticulously hand-pour each candle, ensuring that every product meets our exacting standards.
